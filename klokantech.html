Maps for people.

Klokan Technologies GmbH is a Swiss company specialized in online map publishing, geographic information retrieval and applications of open-source software for the culture heritage sector. The company provides contracted software development, consulting services and participates on research projects world-wide.

MapTiler

Fast maps for the web and for mobile devices.

MapRank Search

Unique technology to explore thousands of maps in time.

Georeferencer

Online service to turn scans into maps. Collaboratively.
