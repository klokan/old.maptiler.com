Drop images of maps into MapTiler and with a couple of clicks the software prepares HTML files with viewers based on Google Maps API, Leaflet and OpenLayers. Enjoy the maps on your mobile phone or tablet even while offline.

Got a map?

Thousands of maps and geodata files are online or you can prepare your own.

Use MapTiler

Open the file, check the geolocation and start rendering of the map tiles.

Publish & View

Copy the result over to a web server, and you’re done! Enjoy your map.

Input formats

TIFF/GeoTIFF, MrSID, ECW, JPEG2000, Erdas HFA, NOAA BSB/KAP, Ozi Explorer OZF2/OZFx3, JPEG, GIF, PNG and more...

Output

Map tiles are compatible with Google Maps, OpenStreetMap, Microsoft Bing, MapQuest, MapBox or with Google Earth. MBTiles format is supported.

Hosting

Apache, PHP, Linux, DropBox, Google Drive, Amazon S3/CloudFront, Google Cloud Storage, Rackspace Cloud Files or other cloud CDNs.

Compatibility

OGC WMTS standard allows to open the maps in several viewers and GIS software, including ArcGIS Desktop, QGIS or UDIG.
