Maps for people.

Maps

Klokan Technologies GmbH was founded in 2010 as a result of almost 10 years of freelance activities of its managing director, Petr Pridal, in the field of online mapping technologies. As an active participant of conferences worldwide, Petr established a network of partners and customers, who share our beliefs in importance of widely accessible culture heritage and maps.

Vision

To connect cartographic past with today’s reality is a path on which we set, building on open-source technologies combined with custom development. A vision for personal use of maps by individuals and growing importance of mobile technologies drives us to create easy-to-use software for end users that combine computing power of their devices with simplicity of use.

Online

The company is privately held with headquarters and the main office in Baar, Switzerland. Second office and a part of the development team are located in Brno, Czech republic. Currently, eight people work for the company and the team actively participates on various research projects internationally and grows with new accepted contracts.
